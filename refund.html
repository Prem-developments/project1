Home Refund Policy
At SilentEYE, we value your satisfaction and strive to provide you with the best service possible. We understand that circumstances may change, and you may need to request a refund. Our Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refund eligibility and the process for requesting a refund.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee (Monthly & Yearly Plans)

We are proud to offer a 30-day, no-questions-asked money-back guarantee for our Monthly & Yearly Plans. If you are not completely satisfied with our services within the first 30 days of your purchase, you are eligible for a full refund. To initiate the refund process, please contact our support team with your refund request.

Weekly Plans

Please note that our Weekly Plan are non-refundable. We encourage our customers to choose the Yearly Plan if they wish to take advantage of our 30-day money-back guarantee.

Eligibility for Refund

To be eligible for a refund under our 30-day money-back guarantee, the following conditions must be met:
1. The refund request must be made within 30 days of the initial purchase.
2. The request must be submitted to our support team.
3. You must have purchased the Monthly or Yearly Plan.

Refund Process

Once your refund request is received and reviewed, our support team will assess whether you meet the eligibility criteria. If your request is approved, we will initiate the refund process. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Please allow 7 Days for the refund to be processed and reflected in your account. If you have any questions or concerns regarding the refund process, you can contact our support team.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding our Refund Policy, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are here to help and ensure your satisfaction.
